Fast Facts

Of these 2 colleges:

  • UC Berkeley is a Significantly Larger School: (37,565 vs. 16,963 Total Students)
  • Stanford is Much More Difficult to Get In: (5.1% vs. 16.0% Acceptance Rate)
  • UC Berkeley Offers Significantly Cheaper In-State Tuition: ($12,972 vs. $45,195)
  • UC Berkeley Offers Considerably Cheaper Out-of-State Tuition: ($35,850 vs. $45,195)
  • Stanford Provides Slightly Better Freshmen Financial Aid: (68% vs. 62% of Eligible Freshmen Receive Aid)
  • Stanford Students Graduate More in 6 Years: (95% vs. 91% Graduation Rate)
  • Stanford Students Earn Much More Salary Post-Graduation: ($60,000 vs. $55,000 Median Salary)
  • Stanford Has Much Smaller Class Sizes: (10:1 vs. 17:1 Student-Teacher Ratio)
  • Stanford Has Slightly Hotter Summer Weather: (67°F vs. 63°F)
  • Stanford Has Slightly Colder Winter Weather: (49°F vs. 51°F)
